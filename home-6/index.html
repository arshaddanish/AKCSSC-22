About AKCSSC'22

All Kerala Computer Society Student Convention is the flagship event of the IEEE Computer Society Kerala Chapter organised every year to inspire IEEE CS Society members to enhance their technical skills and knowledge. AKCSSC'22 is no exception. This year, IEEE Computer Society Kerala Chapter seeks to gather together volunteers from the Computer Society to redefine goals, aspire higher, educate them to be Future Technological Leaders, and make them shed their square edges and fit into the circular hole - the team.

This techno-managerial symposium is an amalgamation of soft skills, networking and professionalism, empowering volunteers in an environment where nothing is inconceivable. It is technology and innovation acting together, catalysed by networking and the art of connecting the whole of Kerala.

AKCSSC '22, the sixth edition of the All Kerala Computer Society Student Convention will be hosted by Mar Baselios Christian College of Engineering, Kuttikkanam, Peermade on November 25 to 27th.

Meet Our Host

Mar Baselios Christian College of Engineering & Technology, Kuttikkanam, Peermade, is a self-financing institution for professional Education, affiliated to Dr APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU) and approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. The prime motive behind IEEE Student Branch at MBCCET is to make the students a part of the largest technological community in the world. It’s a global platform to expose their technical ideas and make connections with great personalities. The weather around the lovely campus is the key feature of the ambience, making the MBCCET Campus rather special for the event. We all love different things about the crisp cold. The cool and temperate climate along with its spacious and welcoming campus makes MBCCET the best host for AKCSSC'22. So let's make the best of what the campus offers while we can!
